Game Data Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2025

What Is Game Data

Game Data includes small pieces of information stored on your device while playing Adventure Capitalist Hole. This data helps us remember your progress, upgrades, and makes your gameplay smoother and more enjoyable.

How We Use Game Data

We use game data for the following purposes:

  • Essential data: Required for the core game features to function properly.
  • Performance data: Helps us analyze gameplay and improve your experience.
  • Personalization data: Remembers your investments and settings.
  • Marketing data: Tracks your gameplay to show relevant offers and updates.

Managing Your Game Data

Most browsers and devices let you control or delete game data. Methods differ, so check your device's settings.
